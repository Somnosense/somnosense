Our Mission

o increase access to early sleep apnea screening through a comfortable, non-invasive intraoral diagnostic system designed for at-home use. We aim to:

  • Reduce barriers such as cost, discomfort, and long wait times
  • Enable screening for individuals who cannot tolerate traditional sleep studies
  • Support earlier intervention and improved long-term health outcomes

Our Vision

We envision a future where sleep apnea screening is simple, affordable, and widely accessible, rather than limited to specialized clinical environments. SomnoSense is designed as a scalable diagnostic platform, progressing from a validated intraoral prototype to a fully miniaturized, biocompatible patch integrated with intelligent data analysis. By combining physical airflow-related signals with biochemical indicators from the oral environment, we aim to bridge the gap between consumer wearables and clinical diagnostics.
