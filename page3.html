Market and Impact

The Problem:
  • Sleep apnea affects over 30 million adults in the US, yet approximately 80% remain undiagnosed
  • Traditional sleep lab testing is expensive, uncomfortable, and difficult to access
  • Delayed diagnosis increases risk of cardiovascular disease and other serious health complications
The Market Opportunity:
  • Rapid growth in at-home, technology-enabled sleep diagnostics
  • US sleep apnea diagnostics and treatment market valued at $7.5B, projected to reach $13B by 2030
Why It Matters:
  • Increases access to early diagnosis
  • Reduces long-term health risks of untreated sleep apnea
  • Reduces strain on sleep labs and healthcare systems
  • Improves quality of life through early intervention
Market Strategy:
  • Direct-to-consumer access for individuals seeking at-home screening
  • Clinical distribution through sleep clinics and hospitals
  • Research partnerships to support validation and credibility
Pricing:
  • Starter kits will be sold at $79-$129 per unit
  • Replacement sensor patches will be sold at $10-$15 per unit
  • The app subscription will be priced at $5-$15 per month
Startup Costs:
  • Product & Sensors: Prototyping, materials, manufacturing
  • Software: App development, AI analytics
  • Clinical & Regulatory: Pilot studies, FDA preparation
  • Operations: Assembly, patents
  • Estimated funding needed: $485K for launch
